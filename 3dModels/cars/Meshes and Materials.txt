Mesh and Material names

BODY_COLORED : The body part which have to colored. All metalic materials. (shiny material)
BODY_NOT_COLORED : The body part which not to be colored. Consists of partially metalic material(shiny) and not shiny materials
- starts with 'DOORLINE' : NOT shiny material. This material is used on doorlines mostly and some other parts.
- starts with 'CHROME' : Metalic material that not have to colored.
GLASS_INTERIOR or GLASS_NO_INTERIOR : All glasses. Material names are also starts with 'GLASS_INTERIOR_ ...' or 'GLASS_NO_INTERIOR_ ...'
INTERIOR (if exists) : Interior modeling. Material name is also 'INTERIOR'.
starts with 'LIGHTS_ ...' : Light modelings.
- starts with 'LIGHT_ ...' : Material that defines light source.
- GLASS_LIGHT : Material that covers the light source.
starts with 'TIRE_ ...' : Modelings of 4 each wheels.
- TIRE : The material of tire.
